We present the invited journal extension of our reject-option work, evaluating per-class binomial thresholds on ImageNet, long-tailed wildlife data, and remote-sensing datasets. The paper adds experimental analysis of class-conditional thresholds, dataset-specific coverage targets, and selective-accuracy reporting for model comparison.
